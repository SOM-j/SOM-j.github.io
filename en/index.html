My Design Philosophy

01

Insight Before Expression

01 / Brand Incubation

JIN QING QINGIndependent Cultural Brand

—10
10 days, zero budget incubate an indie cultural brand from scratch.

Tapping into "neo-mysticism" culture deeply understanding Gen-Z journal enthusiasts' desire for mystique and self-expression. Independently led naming, VI system, 16 SKU designs, supply chain, and Xiaohongshu pre-launch strategy.

Brand Incubation / · Product Design / · Social Strategy / · Community Building /

Gen-Z journaling community craves mysticism and personal expression, but the market lacks products with both aesthetics and cultural depth.

Traditional folk culture is undervalued by young consumers a huge opportunity for "cultural translation" into contemporary visual language.

"Neo-mysticism cultural goods" reconstructing folk symbols with contemporary design, balancing mystique with everyday appeal.

Execution Path

① + Brand naming + VI
② 16 SKU //Product design
③ + Supply chain + production
④ + Pre-launch + offline market debut

03 / Cultural Event

FOTILEFotile Culture Forum

—
Build a visual system merging Eastern philosophy with modern business texture for a high-end cultural forum.

"Neo-Confucian merchant" aesthetic fusing humanism with premium texture. Wang Xizhi's (heart) as the super-symbol, embodying Fotile's "heart-centered" philosophy. Dusky gold tones balance scholarly tradition with modern business.

Super-symbol Design / · Cultural Branding / · New Chinese Aesthetic /

Fotile's core differentiation is "culture-driven" Confucian thought and Eastern philosophy are deep in its DNA. The forum visual must carry this cultural weight, not be a generic corporate event design.

The character from Wang Xizhi's calligraphy as the core symbol the pinnacle of calligraphy art, perfectly resonating with Fotile's "heart-centered" philosophy. Applied across KV, materials, and space for unified cultural identity.

Super-symbol"Heart" throughout all materials
PraisedCultural depth recognized

The visual system was successfully applied across all event materials, creating an elegant cultural atmosphere. The client praised the presentation of "cultural depth" highly.
