witee健康饮 · 海报设计

新锐健康饮品品牌视觉建立——从品牌调性到系列海报全案设计，以自然美学传递"轻养"理念。

Witee Health Drink — brand visual identity & poster series.

海报设计品牌视觉系列延展

witee健康饮定位于"轻养生活"——以天然草本为核心的健康饮品品牌。海报设计围绕"自然、轻盈、日常"三个关键词展开：主视觉以植物剪影与负空间构图，传递成分纯粹感；色彩体系取自晨露与草木的淡绿到暖白渐变，营造温润呼吸感；字体选用圆体搭配手写感标题，平衡专业信赖与亲和力。系列涵盖产品海报、场景海报、社交媒体九宫格及线下终端陈列物料。
Positioned around "light wellness living" — a natural herbal health drink brand. The poster series revolves around three pillars: Nature, Lightness, Daily Ritual. Botanical silhouettes and negative-space compositions convey ingredient purity; a dew-to-warm-white gradient palette creates a breathing, gentle feel; rounded type paired with handwritten headlines balances trust with warmth. Series includes product posters, lifestyle scenes, social media grids, and offline POS materials.

City IP · Character Design

浏阳城市IP形象 · VI设计

取浏阳河青面狮为原型，1:1头身比、圆目腮红、抬手憨态——城市IP"嘿仔"。

"Hei Zai" — Liuyang's Green-faced Lion, reimagined.

为抬憨向南望仔

城市IPVI系统嘿仔

青面狮，浏阳河流域数百年的民间狮舞图腾。设计以此为原点，提炼其神韵入当代IP语言：1:1头身比，舍写实取萌趣；圆目含纯真，腮红添温度，抬手递问候——憨态天成，见之可亲。名之"嘿仔"：一取青面之色，二寓招呼之意，三含湘人爱称之暖——"希望大家会喜欢它"。VI系统覆盖IP规范、城市导视、文创衍生与数字应用。
Rooted in the Green-faced Lion (青面狮), a centuries-old folk totem of the Liuyang River. Distilled into a 1:1 proportion, rounded eyes, soft blush, and a gentle raised hand — an IP that feels at once timeless and tender. Named "Hei Zai": "Hei" for its dark-green visage, echoing a cheerful "hey"; "Zai," Hunan's word for someone beloved. The VI system spans IP guidelines, city wayfinding, cultural merchandise, and digital applications.

Philosophy / 创作理念

My Design Philosophy

不只是视觉——更是品牌与受众之间的情感翻译。
Not just visuals — but the emotional translation between brand and audience.

01

洞察先于表达

Insight Before Expression

每一个设计决策的背后，是对品牌、用户与文化的深度理解。好的设计不是装饰，而是策略的可视化表达——让商业目标拥有情感共鸣的载体。

Behind every design decision lies deep understanding of brand, user, and culture. Good design is not decoration — it's strategy made visible.

02

少即是多，但"少"必须精准

Less Is More, With Precision

克制的色彩、呼吸的留白、精准的字体——简约不是目的，而是剔除一切多余之后剩下的本质。每一像素都有存在的理由。

Restrained colors, breathing whitespace, precise typography — simplicity is not the goal, but the essence that remains when excess is removed.

03

叙事即连接

Narrative Is Connection

品牌与人之间的关系，建立在故事之上。文字、图像、空间——每一种媒介都是叙事的载体，为品牌注入温度、人格与不可替代的灵魂。

The relationship between brand and people is built on stories. Words, images, space — every medium carries narrative, infusing brands with warmth and soul.

Tapping into "neo-mysticism" culture — deeply understanding Gen-Z journal enthusiasts' desire for mystique and self-expression. Independently led naming, VI system, 16 SKU designs, supply chain, and Xiaohongshu pre-launch strategy.

Brand Incubation / 品牌孵化 · Product Design / 产品设计 · Social Strategy / 社交策略 · Community Building / 社群运营

Gen-Z journaling community craves mysticism and personal expression, but the market lacks products with both aesthetics and cultural depth.

色彩系统 Color System：黛金（dusky gold）为主色——既有东方美学的贵气与内敛，又符合现代商业的质感要求。
